A Few Words About Me

Hey, I'm Case! I'm a senior English major here at the University of Northern Colorado in Greeley. I currently work as a materials page at Farr Regional Library. I plan to become a full librarian someday.

Research Interests

My research topics of interest are literature, music, art history, mythology, folklore, and world religions.

Professtional Interests

I'm interested in the ways that I can help individuals foster their own love of learning. As a writer, I'm constantly pushing myself to grow, and I believe that the environment created by libraries is one of constant growth, not stagnation.

More About Me

As stated above, I'm a writer, and, naturally, an avid reader. My favorite format is poetry, but I also occasionally write flash fiction and vignettes. I also enjoy hands-on projects like gardening, drawing, origami, and other crafts.
